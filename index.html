WHO Guidelines & FAQ

Frequently asked questions about mental health and our platform

What is mental health according to WHO? +
According to the World Health Organization, mental health is "a state of well-being in which every individual realizes his or her own potential, can cope with the normal stresses of life, can work productively and fruitfully, and is able to make a contribution to her or his community."
How does AI support mental health? +
AI can provide 24/7 accessibility, personalized responses, and consistent support while maintaining privacy. However, it should complement, not replace, professional mental healthcare.
When should I seek professional help? +
Seek professional help if you experience persistent sadness, anxiety, thoughts of self-harm, substance abuse, or if mental health issues interfere with daily functioning for more than two weeks.
Is my data secure on MindSpace? +
Yes, we use end-to-end encryption and follow HIPAA guidelines. Your conversations and personal data are stored securely and never shared without your explicit consent.
What makes a good mental health support system? +
A good support system includes: accessibility, non-judgmental environment, professional resources, peer support, crisis intervention, and personalized care approaches.
